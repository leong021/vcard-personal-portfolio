About me

I'm 3D Modeler/animator from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, working in project management and 3D modelling. I enjoy making 3D character and creature models. I am also doing cosplay photography as a hobby/part time.

This portfolio website is a curated collection of my work, spanning various projects and styles! From whimsical characters to sleek architectural designs, each piece reflects my passion for creativity and attention to detail. As you navigate through the pages, you'll discover the depth of my experience and the breadth of my skills. Whether I'm crafting lifelike creatures or visualizing intricate structures, my goal is always the same: to evoke emotion and captivate the viewer.
